Who is Melissa Bachman?

Melissa Bachman is an American huntress and Television host. Currently, she runs the television program Winchester Deadly Passion on the Sportsman Channel, Pursuit Channel, and Wild TV. Moreover, she also owns a production company named Deadly Passion Productions.

Melissa Bachman: Early Life, Childhood, Education

Melissa was born in 1984 in Paynesville, Minnesota, United States. She is the daughter of Dale Bachman(father) and Karen Bachman(mother).

Talking about her early life, she grew up in Paynesville, Minnesota along with her parents. Furthermore, her parents were also hunters. There is no information about her siblings, she seems like a single child.

Talking about his childhood, she had a keen interest in hunting. As a child, Melissa began hunting at the early age of five. Moving towards her education, she attended Paynesville Area High School. Later, she completed her graduation in Spanish and Broadcast Journalism from St. Cloud State University.

Melissa Bachman began her hunting career as an unpaid internship with North American Media Group’s North American Hunter Television in Minnetonka. At that time, she also worked as a waitress at a nightclub.

After four months, she was hired as a full-time producer for the show. During that time, Melissa served as a camera professional. Furthermore, Melissa Bachman also shot, produced, and edited the first season of Mark Kayser’s Extreme Pursuits program.

In 2010, the veteran huntress launched her own production company named Deadly Passion Productions. Back in 2012, Melissa Bachman made her comeback o North American Hunter as a host.
