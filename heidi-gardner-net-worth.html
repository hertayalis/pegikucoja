Heidi Gardner is an American actress and comedian. She first appeared in the 2014 independent film Dibs! On television, she debuted as the voice of Jasmine in the animated series Bratz. She next appeared in the 2018 feature film Life of the Party. That same year, she appeared in the movie Making Babies. More recently, she had one-time roles in The Other Two and Veep.
