The Rise To Fame With "Panda" & Collaboration With Kanye West

Desiigner exploded onto the music scene in 2016 with his infectious track "Panda." The song's success was stratospheric, topping charts globally and earning platinum status multiple times. Its popularity soared further when Kanye West sampled it for his album The Life Of Pablo. As a result, it drew widespread attention to Desiigner's distinctive style and flow.

This collaboration led to Desiigner signing with Kanye's label, G.O.O.D Music, marking a pivotal moment in his career. The partnership with the iconic rapper and producer propelled Desiigner into the spotlight. Through this, he was presented with numerous opportunities in the competitive music industry.

Despite the initial success and the exposure brought by G.O.O.D Music, Desiigner eventually parted ways with the label. The decision to depart from Kanye's imprint was a strategic move. This departure from the label, while unexpected for some, showcased Desiigner's determination to carve his own creative journey. As he navigates the industry independently, Desiigner continues to explore new musical horizons, showcasing his artistic growth and evolving sound beyond the confines of a major label.
