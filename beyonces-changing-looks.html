Beyonce's Changing Looks

May 2024 · 3 minute read

See Beyonce's transformation through the years.

1996

The freshman at the High School for the Performing and Visual Arts in Houston was already a part of Destiny's Child.

1998

The Destiny's Child lead took beauty cues from her mother: "She made sure we had our lip gloss or blush in our purses. She made us pay attention to the details."

2000

While on tour with Destiny's Child, Knowles joined the ranks of blond bombshells like her idol Marilyn Monroe.

2001

For Destiny's Child's Survivor, the braided beauty wrote the song that changed the English language forever: "Look, I'm proud that [bootylicious] is in the dictionary, but it drives me nuts," she has said. "Change it to 'beautylicious.'"

2002

She went big, bold and two-toned for her first movie role, as Foxxy Cleopatra in Austin Powers in Goldmember.

2003

"My favorite look on her is bone-straight," hairstylist Kimberly Kimble has said of the Dangerously in Love singer.

2004

At Grammys for the last time as a member of Destiny's Child, she wore her caramel curls in a loose chignon.

2005

Beyoncé launched her new fragrance, True Star Gold, wearing her hair in lush waves.

2005

She walked the red carpet at the Academy Awards with an Old Hollywood-inspired bouffant ponytail and dramatic liquid liner and lashes.

2006

For the premiere of The Pink Panther, Beyonce chose romantic finger waves and appropriately rosy lips.

2007

Making a rare switch from blond, she partied in Las Vegas with ebony waves softened by brunet highlights.

2008

Beyoncé rocked a center part and mega curls at a NYC club opening.

2008

Beyoncé had stick-straight locks on the cover of the November issue of InStyle.

2009

When it came to promoting her double album I Am...Sasha Fierce, Beyonce went for an understated coif: "I've had so many hairstyles and I don't want this project to be about my hair."

2010

Beyoncé rocked tinsel highlights at this year's Grammys.

2010

Gorgeous and philanthropic, she opened the Beyoncé Cosmetology Center at the Phoenix House Residential Rehab Clinic in Brooklyn rocking trendy ombre locks.

2011

She flaunted a major mane of bouncy curls at a Harlem event.

2011

The mama-to-be sported a sexy side-braid at the launch of her Pulse fragrance.

2012

Blue Ivy's mama chose sleek, swept-back hair and a persimmon lip color for her visit to the Costume Institute Gala.

2012

Beyonce debuted bangs and a new hue at a Brooklyn Nets game. "It took me a few hours to get her to that butterscotch color and keep it healthy, that was my goal," colorist Rita Hazan told InStyle.com. "It's warm, but beige-y-I call it a beautiful Beyonce blonde!"

2013

In 2013, Bey stepped out with an asymmetrical bob for a concert in Philadelphia.

2014

That was fast! The singer returned to her signature long mane after briefly experimenting with shorter hairstyles.

2015

Queen Bey debuted a chic, new look at the 2015 Vanity Fair Oscar party—proving, once again, that she can do no wrong.

2016

To attend the Met Gala, which was themed "Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology," Beyoncé rocked hip-length pin-straight strands with striking highlights and a seriously bold smoky eye.

2017

Queen Bey showed off her pregnancy glow and tasteful décolletage at the Grammys in February.
