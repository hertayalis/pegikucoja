20 baby shower themes: from a pamper party to botanicals

July 2024 · 7 minute read

Baby showers are one of the most fun and special ways to celebrate a mum-to-be and her impending arrival, and the perfect excuse to go all out with themed décor, games and food.

But with so much choice, what theme should you go with and where should you get started? Of course, one of the easiest places to start would be with the baby's gender and traditional pink and blue colours if the mum-to-be already knows what she's having. What is needed to plan a baby shower?

Three of the first things you need to plan a baby shower are a time and date, a location to host it, and a guest list. From there, you can start thinking about the theme for your baby shower, and design or order invitations to suit. 

Once your theme has been secured and invitations have been sent, you can start thinking about finding any decorations you need, planning refreshments and thinking of baby shower cake ideas to match.

What is a normal budget for a baby shower?

A 2019 survey by American Express found that the average cost of a baby shower in the UK was £91, with guests spending an average of £38 each on gifts. However, this cost will no doubt have increased over the past few years due to inflation. The budget will also depend on whether you book a private venue to host your celebration or have a more low-key baby shower at home, along with the number of guests you invite and what you have planned in terms of catering.
