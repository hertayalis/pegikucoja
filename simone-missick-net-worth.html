September 2024 · 1 minute read

Simone Missick

$1 million

Simone Missick is an American actress whose career started in the 2000s. She first appeared in the 2003 film The Epicureans. In the 2010s, she made guest roles in the TV shows Bucket & Skinner’s Epic Adventures, Ray Donovan, Scandal, and Wayward Pines. She also starred in the TV movie A Taste of Romance. From 2016 to 2018, she became known for her role as Misty Knight in the Netflix series Luke Cage. She reprised her role in The Defenders and Iron Fist. Afterward, she appeared in 2 episodes of Mariana Reynolds in the web series Tell Me a Story. As of 2019, she stars as Lola Carmichael in the CBS series All Rise. Also, she recently starred as Trepp in Altered Carbon.
